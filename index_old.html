Frequently Asked Questions.


How does this work?


It's easy. You simply click "Sign Up & Earn" then enter your email address and payment information".


This will trigger emails that contain links to surveys and offers that you can complete.


Once you've completed a survey or offer our system will notify us that you completed it in good faith.


This means the survey/offer provider paid us, so we can in turn pay you.



Payments are disbursed to your preferred payment method within 24-72 Hours for each survey/offer.



How much do I earn per survey/offer? 


Earnings are variable based on geographic location.


All users with preferred payment methods as crypto address


﻿will have their offers converted from USD to that specific crypto.


The amount of crypto you will receive is variable to the price at the time of sending payment. 


Not every country will have surveys or offers available.


This means some users may not be able to earn.


If that is the case for you please use the "Get Support" button at the top of this page.



How often are payments made?


Payments are disbursed to your HNS Wallet Address within 24-72 Hours.



Can I keep track of how many surveys I've completed?


You will receive email confirmations each time you complete an offer.


You can also login to view your completions.


How do you stop scammers from ruining this?


We use click protection/tracking platform ClickMagick
