Frequently Asked Questions.


How does this work?


It's easy. You simply enter your payment information above and click "Start Survey".


It will automatically redirect you to the page to where you can complete surveys to earn HNS.


Once you've completed a survey our system will notify us if you completed the survey in good faith. This means the survey provider paid us, so we can in turn pay you.


Payments are disbursed to your selected payment option within 24-72 Hours for each survey.

How often are payments made?


Payments are disbursed to your HNS Wallet Address within 24-72 Hours.
